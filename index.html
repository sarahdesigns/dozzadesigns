My Life

What I do now

I am Sarah, a graphic designer who specializes in web development. Currently, I manage all my web development projects from start to finish. This includes the initial research, proposals, wireframes, designs, development and testing.

I also work on various other projects while managing my web devs, like email marketing campaigns, digital ad designs and print work.

What I want to do more of

I create sites based on a client’s needs, objectives and current design trends. A site can look beautiful, but will that same site create an easy experience for the user to navigate through, and find the information he/she is looking for?

This is the area of web design I want to work more closely in, User Experience Design. Whether is it an application, website or e-commerce site development, I want to play a larger role in the research and testing that goes along with creating the ultimate user experience.
